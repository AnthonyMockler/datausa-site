DataUSA in Action

DataUSA can take tasks that previously took weeks or months of research and shorten them to a few minutes of user-friendly clicks and searches. For example, DataUSA can help answer questions in the following scenarios:

Opening a new office or relocating a business. Which locations have the kind of talent, education levels, and skills that I need? What are the most affordable options?

Expanding a product to new markets. What does my customer profile look like within any particular geographic region? Is there synergy with local industries to develop/distribute my product?

Enrolling as a college student. Iâ€™d like to major in chemistry or philosophy. What types of jobs do these or other majors lead me to? Where are the best places to live for the job I would like to have?

Facing economic complexity. I work at the Chamber of Commerce in a city that is relatively specialized, and I want to understand what industries my city supplies to reduce our vulnerability to economic shocks. What industries are using our products that would create vulnerabilities in our community? What occupations are most responsible?
